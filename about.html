About us

Hi, I'm Paul.

I built Halfpenny Mac because I wanted a Mac in the cloud that was actually in the UK — and didn't cost a fortune.

A while back I needed a persistent Mac for an iOS CI pipeline. The obvious options were all US-based — MacStadium, MacInCloud, AWS EC2 Mac instances. Fine services, but you're paying US prices, dealing with US latency from the UK, and your data lives on the other side of the Atlantic. It felt wrong.

I'm a developer based in the Cotswolds. I already had a couple of Mac minis running reliably at home and had started lending one to a friend for their Xcode builds. That arrangement worked surprisingly well. The minis are quiet, power-efficient, and Apple silicon is genuinely fast for the kind of work developers do — builds, local AI inference, browser automations. It seemed worth doing properly.

Halfpenny Mac is the result. Dedicated hardware, UK-based, managed by me. No shared kernels, no virtual machines, no noisy neighbours. You get a real Mac mini with a proper managed setup — Tailscale for access, FileVault for encryption, nightly snapshots to Backblaze — and I keep the plumbing running so you don't have to.

This isn't an enterprise product. There's no sales team, no SLA with financial penalties, no account managers. If something breaks, you email me and I fix it. That's the deal. I think that's actually better for most of the people who'd want this: indie developers, small iOS teams, AI tinkerers who want something that just works, not a contract to negotiate.

If that sounds like you, I'd love to have you as a customer. The waitlist is the starting point — we're growing slowly on purpose, to keep quality high.
