5. Data handling and security

Encryption at rest

All Mac minis have FileVault full-disk encryption enabled and enforced via MDM. Recovery keys are escrowed to our management system, not to iCloud.

Backups and snapshots

We take a nightly snapshot of your home directory (not full-disk images) and store it encrypted on Backblaze B2. Snapshots are retained for 14 rolling days. You can request a restore via a support ticket; we will aim to complete restores within 4 hours during business hours.

Snapshots are provided for disaster recovery. They are not a substitute for your own backup strategy for critical data.

Access

Your Mac mini is yours during your subscription. We retain a separate admin account for maintenance, updates, and abuse response. We will not access your account or data except:

  • To perform routine maintenance (patching, hardware checks) with reasonable notice;
  • In response to an abuse report or suspected policy violation;
  • As required by law or court order.

Data on termination

On account termination (whether by you or by us), your Mac mini is wiped using a secure erase process. Backups associated with your account are deleted within 14 days of termination. We do not retain copies of your data beyond this period.
